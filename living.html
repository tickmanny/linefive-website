What types of properties does Linefive offer?

We offer a range of high-quality short-term rental properties in Tema and Accra, catering to various needs and preferences. These properties include modern apartments, spacious townhouses, and luxury villas, each thoughtfully designed to provide comfort, style, and convenience. Whether guests are in Ghana for business, vacation, or a short stay,our properties are fully furnished and equipped with essential amenities, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable experience in two of Ghana’s most vibrant cities.

How do I schedule a property tour?

To schedule a property tour, simply reach out through the contact section below on our website. Click here to get in touch, and our team will be happy to assist you in arranging a visit.

Are Linefive homes pet-friendly?

Yes,our homes are pet-friendly! We welcome your furry companions so you can enjoy your stay together. Please let us know in advance if you'll be bringing a pet, and we’ll provide all the necessary information for a comfortable experience.

How do I book with Linefive?

Getting started with us is easy! You can book through our listings on Airbnb for a seamless reservation process, or contact us directly for personalized assistance and booking options. We're here to help you find the perfect stay.

Can I purchase an Linefive property instead of renting?

Currently, our properties are not available for long-term rental or purchase. However, we encourage you to express your interest, as new projects and opportunities are on the way. Stay connected for updates!
